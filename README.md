## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 27,979 delivery 
orders across 6 Texas hubs — tracking on-time performance, 
driver efficiency, vehicle health, and customer satisfaction 
for a logistics & delivery operations business.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- Overall On-Time Delivery Rate: 78.88%
- 21.12% orders were delayed — top reason: Road 
  Construction & Vehicle Breakdown
- El Paso Hub has best performance (80.6% on-time)
- 26% of fleet (12 vehicles) currently in maintenance
- Avg Customer Satisfaction: 4.17 out of 5
- Vans handle 62% of all deliveries

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards — Total Orders, On-Time %, Avg Satisfaction,
  Avg Delivery Time
- Hub-wise performance comparison
- Delay reason breakdown chart
- Vehicle type & status analysis
- Driver performance & employment type view
- Order status (Delivered vs Cancelled)
- Customer satisfaction by hub
